by commissioning me you agree to the terms of service.

i only do digital commissions

the commissions will be done in your desired art style, and i can do portraits, landscape, realism, character design, oc, fanart, episode cover art and more.

how it works

  • contact me via instagram dms and give me a detailed description of what you want for the illustration. specify which style you want, too. feel free to send me photos for reference.

  • after accepting your commission, i may request further details and payment which must be upfront

  • i will be sending a sketch to revise with you a few days after payment.

  • average wait time is 1-3 weeks for the finished illustration. understand that this may vary as i am a fulltime college student.

  • i will send the final work as a PNG via email.

terms of service

general terms

  • you will receive 2 versions of the illustration - one watermarked and one without watermark.

  • water marked version: can be used freely with optional credit.

  • non-watermarked version: personal use only, unless you want the commercial option which is +50% of the full price.

  • i reserve the right to post the commission on my socials my portfolio.

refusal

i reserve the right to decline anything that makes me uncomfortable or anything i deem i’m not capable of.

immediate declines are

  • nudity, nsfw, gore

payment

  • payment in DKK via paypal.

  • payment 100% upfront.

  • no refunds after i have started the commission.

process and revision

  • after payment is received i will make a rough sketch which i will share with you and you can make unlimited changes from pose to composition. once i start the lineart and colors, small detail changes can be done. major changes after that will be charged for.

  • i may refuse any changes i don’t wish to make.

  • i will update you on the commission every few days/weeks. i work on more than one commission at the same time so updates may be slow. you can also see the process on my trello board.

  • the final work will be sent as a PNG via email. you are only purchasing a digital file, not printed art.

copyright and usage policy

  • i, the artist, reserve all rights to any produced artwork. i own the rights to:

  • uploading your commission to any of my socials, portfolio and post speedpaints of it, or use as self-promotion.

  • reuse the pose or composition of the artwork with some changes but not the character itself (for oc).

  • the client is allowed to use the piece for personal use ONLY unless specified otherwise in our contract. this includes posting to their own social media, printing the artwork for themselves, all as long as no monetary profit is earned from the artwork.

  • the client is NOT allowed to reproduce, modify, or use the artwork in any way other than personal. this includes NFTs, prints, stickers, merchandise, etc.

  • the client is NOT allowed to resell or use my art for tracing and then claiming it as an original. do not claim as your own.

  • breaking any of these points will result in copyright infringement and you will be banned from commissioning me again.

cancelation policy/refunds

  • a refund cannot be requested after i start the commission.

  • if i cannot complete the commission for any reason, i will offer a full refund or an alternative artwork.

  • do not request a charge back; i am handling the refunds myself.

  • requesting a chargeback after digital delivery via email will be declined, and your commission will be canceled. i will be obligated to share to paypal our conversation as proof that the commission was delivered and that ToS have been broken from your end.

  • you will also be blacklisted and banned from ever commissioning me again, as well as publicly exposed to warn fellow artists.

  • claiming dissatisfaction with the final work does not warrant a refund, as the client has multiple opportunities throughout the process to voice their concerns. doing this will also result in being blacklisted.




if you agree to all of the above, you are now free to dm me!
